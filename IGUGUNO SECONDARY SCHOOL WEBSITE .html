About Us

Iguguno Secondary School is located in Mkalama District, Singida Region, Tanzania. The school offers quality secondary education from Form One to Form Six. Our curriculum includes a variety of subjects to ensure our students receive a well-rounded education. We are committed to providing an environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.
